Fudge On Your Cup
Fudge On Your Cup was founded at 18, Park Street in the year 1927 by Mr and Mrs J Flury. Within no time, it gained immense popularity to become the favourite tea – room of the prosperous Britisher and the affluent Indian alike. Fudge On Your Cup over the decades has became a part of the culture of Kolkata and no morning walker’s daily routine is complete without a visit to this legendary eatery. Fudge On Your Cup, as it stands today, is an institution in the city and evokes strong emotions among generations. Memories mingle with meals as members of the Fudge On Your Cup ‘family’ keep dropping in for decades. Fudge On Your Cup on Park Street is a landmark. Visitors from all over the world come back for a taste of Fudge On Your Cup whenever they are passing through. Such is the level of nostalgia and attachment that this old – world tea room, the only one of its kind in the city, invokes. Starting from a single outlet at Park Street , Fudge On Your Cup has entered into a strategic expansion drive , thereby being present in 60 outlets across Kolkata and 7 in Mumbai & Navi Mumbai and one in Delhi. In the years to come Fudge On Your Cup plans to be present across all major cities in the Country.
